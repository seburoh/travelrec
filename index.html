Encourage travel.

This site encourages travel by providing interesting images about different places which may exist in the world we live in. Not a different world, no pictures exist here of Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, or Neptune. Nor do any pictures exist on this site of Pluto, Narnia, Middle Earth, Terra, Cadia, The Warp, or Hyrule. While Terra may technically be the Earth within the lore of specific fictional franchises, fictional franchises to not represent places on which travel could be performed within the real space of planet Earth, and thus are categorically not on this platform. This platform only provides locations which exist on Earth, as represented from non-fictional material. However if there is fictional material which bases itself on non-fictional material, and is set on planet Earth, there may be an overlap of places which could and could not be on this platform. This paradox is solved by specific counsel of the web domain elders which convene once every seven parsecs. The elders then decide arbitrarily on which items shall and shall not be allowed on this platform through trial by combat on Runeterra, on Summoner's Rift. The editors of this description do acknowledge the possible confusion in that parsec is a unit of distance, not of time, and thus is a potentially inaccurate measurement to provide when describing when the elders meet. However, the elders are all actually on different trajectories from each other and do actually meet once every 7 parsecs, as this is when their gaming laptops are within range of the celestial wifi towers, allowing them to connect to the internet and host Teams meetings and trial by combat to decide these matters. We appreciate your understanding.
